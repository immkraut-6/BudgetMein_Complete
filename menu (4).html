🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2025 | Effective for all BudgetMein customers

1. Who We Are

BudgetMein is a home-based food delivery service operating in Jahangir Puri, Delhi — 110033. We are not a restaurant chain or corporation; we are a small local business committed to serving budget-friendly food to our community. You can contact us via WhatsApp or phone as listed on our website.

2. Information We Collect

When you sign up or place an order with BudgetMein, we collect the following information:

  • Full Name — to address you and confirm your order.
  • Phone Number — to contact you regarding your delivery.
  • Email Address — for account login and order history.
  • Delivery Address — to deliver your food to the correct location.
  • Order Details — items ordered, total amount, order time.
  • Feedback — ratings and comments you voluntarily submit.

We do not collect payment card details. All orders are Cash on Delivery (COD).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your food orders.
  • To notify you about your order status.
  • To maintain your order history for re-ordering convenience.
  • To send internal order notifications via Telegram (to our team only).
  • To improve our food quality and service based on your feedback.
  • To contact you in case of delivery issues.

We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing, third-party advertising, or any purpose unrelated to fulfilling your order.

4. Data Storage

Your account data (name, phone, email) is stored securely using Firebase Authentication and Firestore provided by Google. Order history is stored locally on your device (browser localStorage) and optionally synced to our Firestore database. We take reasonable precautions to protect your data from unauthorized access.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for commercial purposes. Your information is only shared with:

  • Our delivery team — name, phone, and address needed to complete your delivery.
  • Firebase/Google — for secure account management (subject to Google's Privacy Policy).

6. Your Rights

  • You can update your name, phone number, and delivery address anytime from your Profile.
  • You can change your account password from your Profile.
  • You can delete your order history from the "My Orders" section.
  • To request full account deletion, contact us via WhatsApp.

7. Cookies & Local Storage

BudgetMein uses browser localStorage to save your cart, address, and order history for a better experience. No advertising cookies are used. Firebase may use cookies for authentication — these are necessary for your account to function.

8. Children's Privacy

BudgetMein services are intended for individuals aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our service or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of BudgetMein after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For any privacy concerns, data requests, or questions, please contact us:
